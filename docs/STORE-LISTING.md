## Full description (Play / iOS description)
```
Feel looser, move easier — in just a few minutes a day.

StretchHome is a free, guided stretching app for your whole body, designed to be
done at home with things you already have: a chair, a wall, a table, or nothing at all.
No gym, no equipment, no subscription — and it works for every age, from busy desk
workers to active athletes to seniors.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
• Completely free — no paywall, no trial, no premium lock
• Stretch by body part — neck, shoulders, back, hips, hamstrings, calves and more
• Clear step-by-step guidance with breathing cues and safety tips
• A simple hold-timer so you always know how long to stay
• Guided routines that flow from one stretch to the next, hands-free
• Gentle, standard, and deeper options for every stretch
• Works offline — stretch anywhere, anytime
• Easy on the eyes: calming design, dark mode, and a large-text mode

PERFECT FOR
• Desk workers easing neck, shoulder and back tension
• Seniors and beginners who want safe, supported stretches
• Anyone warming up, cooling down, or just feeling stiff
• Better posture, flexibility, and everyday mobility

ROUTINES INCLUDED
Desk Reset • Full Body Daily • Gentle for Seniors • Morning Wake-Up •
Bedtime Wind-Down • Neck & Shoulder Relief • Hip Opener Flow • Posture Fix • and more.

Stretching only — no complicated workouts, no pressure. Just a calmer, looser you.

Note: StretchHome offers general fitness information, not medical advice. Move gently
and stop if you feel pain. Consult a doctor if you have an injury or health condition.
```
