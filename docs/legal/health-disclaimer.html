Before you begin

Consult a doctor or qualified professional before starting this or any exercise program, especially if you are pregnant, recovering from injury or surgery, have a heart condition, high or low blood pressure, joint problems, balance issues, or any other medical condition, or if you are new to physical activity.

While you stretch

  • Move slowly and stay within a comfortable, pain-free range.
  • Never bounce or force a stretch.
  • Stop immediately if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, numbness, chest pain, or shortness of breath, and seek medical attention if symptoms persist.
  • Use a stable chair or wall for support where suggested, to avoid falls.
  • Make sure you have a clear, non-slip space.
