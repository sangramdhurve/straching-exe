Privacy Policy — StretchHome

Effective date: 14 June 2026 · Last updated: 14 June 2026

StretchHome ("the app", "we", "us") is a free stretching app provided by Sangram ("the developer"). This policy explains what information the app handles and how. By using StretchHome you agree to this policy.

1. Summary

StretchHome does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, or password. All your in-app data (favorites, settings, streak) is stored only on your device. The app shows advertisements through Google AdMob, which may use a mobile advertising identifier to serve ads.

2. Information we handle

Stored on your device only (not sent to us): your saved favorite stretches, display preferences (theme, large-text), and your stretch streak. This data stays on your phone and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Collected by our advertising and analytics partners: to show ads and understand basic app usage, third-party services may collect a device advertising identifier, approximate (non-precise) location derived from IP, device type and operating system, and ad-interaction events. We do not combine this with any identity because we do not collect your identity.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may use device identifiers and usage data to provide and personalize ads. You can learn how Google uses this data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. You can opt out of ad personalization in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising).

4. Analytics

We may use Google Firebase Analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage (for example, which routines are popular) so we can improve the app. This does not identify you personally. See Firebase privacy information.

5. Children's privacy

StretchHome is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it. (Younger children may use the app under adult supervision; the content itself is suitable for all ages.)

6. How information is used

To operate the app, display advertising that keeps it free, and improve the experience through aggregate analytics. We do not sell your personal information.

7. Data sharing

We share data only with the service providers described above (Google AdMob, Google Play services, and Firebase) for the purposes described, or where required by law.

8. Your choices & rights

You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings, opt out of ad personalization, and clear all on-device data by uninstalling the app. Depending on your region (e.g. EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access or delete personal data; contact us to exercise them.

9. Data security & retention

On-device data remains under your control on your device. Data handled by our partners is retained according to their own policies. We keep no personal user database of our own.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, provide notice in the app.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy: support@garibaitservices.com.
